Exploring Personality Types Through MBTI

MBTI is a tool that identifies and classifies individuals into specific personality types by assessing their preferences in four dichotomies: Extraversion/Introversion, Sensing/iNtuition, Thinking/Feeling, and Judging/Perceiving. It offers a framework for understanding behaviors, communication styles, and decision-making processes.

Personality Types

INTJ I-Introverted, N-Intuitive, T-Thinking, J-Judging

Strategic and independent thinkers, driven by innovation, known for their vision and problem-solving abilities.

INTP I-Introverted, N-Intuitive, T-Thinking, P-Perceiving

Innovative and curious, seeks understanding and knowledge, excels in abstract thinking.

ENTJ E-Extroverted, N-Intuitive, T-Thinking, J-Judging

Confident and decisive leaders, strong-willed and strategic, focused on long-term goals and efficient systems.

ENTP E-Extroverted, N-Intuitive, T-Thinking, P-Perceiving

Resourceful and intellectually quick, enjoys debating ideas, driven by a desire to explore new concepts.

INFJ I-Introverted, N-Intuitive, F-Feeling, J-Judging

INFP I-Introverted, N-Intuitive, F-Feeling, P-Perceiving

ENFJ E-Extraverted, N-Intuitive, F-Feeling, J-Judging
